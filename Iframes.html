Sandbox:

The sandbox attribute provides security restrictions for the content within the iframe. It prevents certain actions like form submissions, script execution, and popups.

Scrollable

  1. It's content that extends beyond the visible area of a screen or page.
  2. To view the entire content, you need to move it up, down, left, or right.

allowfullscreen

allowfullscreen is a simple word that means "let it go full screen". It's like asking for permission to make something big and fill the whole screen.

Viewport

Viewport is like a window on your phone or computer screen where you see a website. It tells the website how big that window is, so it can fit itself nicely without being too small or too big.
